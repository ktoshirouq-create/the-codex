LEGAL REGS (SERVING)

AGE REQUIREMENTS

  • Must be 18+ to buy/serve Groups 1 & 2.
  • Must be 20+ to buy/serve Group 3 (Spirits).
  • Staff must demand ID in any case of doubt.
  • Ensure minors do not drink alcohol served to others.

INTOXICATION & DISCOUNTS

  • Forbidden to serve anyone "obviously under the influence".
  • Forbidden to serve alcohol in a manner that assumes the person will become obviously under the influence.
  • Group Rule: If one in a group is drunk, nobody in that group gets served until the drunk person is removed.
  • Offering special discounts on alcohol to consumers is strictly prohibited.

HOUSE RULES

  • No consumption of outside alcohol on premises.
  • No taking drinks away from the bar area.
  • Total Advertising Ban: The advertising of alcoholic beverages is completely prohibited.

SERVING TIMES (NATIONAL)

  • Spirits (Cat 3): Allowed 13:00 to 24:00. Strictly prohibited 03:00 to 13:00.
  • Beer/Wine: Allowed 08:00 to 01:00. Strictly prohibited 03:00 to 06:00.
  • Clearance: All alcohol consumption must cease no later than 30 mins after serving hours expire.
