ALKOHOLLOVEN (THE ALCOHOL ACT)

SERVING TIMES (SKJENKETIDER)

  • For beer and wine (under 22% ABV), the national normal serving time is 08:00 to 01:00.
  • For spirits (22% ABV and above), the national normal serving time is 13:00 to 24:00.
  • Municipalities can extend these times, but serving alcohol is strictly forbidden nationwide between 03:00 and 06:00.
  • For spirits, the absolute nationwide ban on serving is between 03:00 and 13:00.
  • All consumption of poured alcohol must cease no later than 30 minutes after the serving time expires.

RETAIL SALES (SALGSTIDER)

  • On weekdays (Monday to Friday), the maximum time municipalities can allow supermarket alcohol sales is until 20:00.
  • On Saturdays and days before public holidays, the maximum time for supermarket alcohol sales is 18:00.
  • Alcohol sales are completely prohibited on Sundays and major public holidays.

PENALTIES & PRIKKSYSTEMET

  • If a venue is allocated 12 penalty points within a 2-year period, the liquor license shall be revoked for a duration of one week.
  • If more than 12 points are allocated, the length of the license revocation is increased correspondingly.
  • The licensee can be subjected to penalties and held responsible for damages under the Alcohol Act.
  • The licensee must ensure that volunteers/employees know about the regulations and comply with them.
